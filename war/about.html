Ugolki is a board game originating from Russia. It is a sort of simplified version of Chinese checkers.
The beauty of this game that it has very simple rules but allows enough depth for strategical challenge. It's also easy going and usually takes only about 5-10 minutes per game.
I decided to create one and put on the web - for free - so you can play it versus computer player or versus human player that is sharing your computer. There's also an Google Wave version of Ugolki - it allows human vs. human mode on remote computers based on the Wave platform. Look for it in the Wave Extensions Gallery.
